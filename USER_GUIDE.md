## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit Keja**: Go to the Keja website
2. **Click "Sign Up"**: Located in the top-right corner
3. **Choose Account Type**:
   - **Regular User**: For people looking to rent properties
   - **Realtor**: For property managers and real estate agents
4. **Fill in Your Details**:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Secure password
5. **Verify Your Account**: Check your email for verification

### Signing In

1. **Click "Sign In"** from the homepage
2. **Enter Your Credentials**: Email and password
3. **Access Your Dashboard**: Automatically redirected based on your account type

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is Keja free to use?**
A: Yes! Creating an account and searching for properties is completely free for renters.

**Q: How do I know if a property is still available?**
A: All listings show real-time availability. Properties marked as "Active" are currently available for rent.

**Q: Can I view properties without an appointment?**
A: We recommend booking appointments to ensure the property agent is available and prepared for your visit.

### Account & Security

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

**Q: Is my personal information secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal information without consent.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, contact our support team to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

### Property Search

**Q: How often are new properties added?**
A: New properties are added daily. Set up search alerts to be notified of new matches.

**Q: Can I search for properties outside Nairobi?**
A: Yes! Keja covers properties across Kenya, including Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and other major cities.

**Q: What if I can't find what I'm looking for?**
A: Use our "Request Property" feature to let agents know what you're seeking.

### Appointments & Viewings

**Q: What should I bring to a property viewing?**
A: Bring a valid ID, proof of income, and any questions you have about the property.

**Q: Can I bring someone with me to viewings?**
A: Yes, you can bring family members or friends to help you make decisions.

**Q: What happens if I'm late to an appointment?**
A: Contact the agent immediately. Most agents can accommodate small delays.

### For Realtors

**Q: How much does it cost to list properties?**
A: We offer free basic listings and premium plans with additional features.

**Q: How do I get more visibility for my properties?**
A: Upgrade to premium plans for featured listings, priority placement, and advanced analytics.

**Q: Can I manage multiple properties?**
A: Yes, our platform supports unlimited property listings for realtors.
